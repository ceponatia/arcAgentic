# Welcome to ArcAgentic

Welcome to the ArcAgentic documentation! This guide will help you run the app on your own machine and create rich, interactive NPCs and immersive settings for your role-playing experiences.

## What is ArcAgentic?

ArcAgentic is a platform for creating and interacting with AI-powered non-player characters (NPCs) in custom settings. Build detailed character profiles, design unique worlds, and engage in dynamic conversations that bring your stories to life.

If you have not started the app yet, begin with the [Self-Hosting guide](#/docs/self-hosting) to get everything running.

## Key Features

### [Character Builder](#/docs/character-builder)

Create rich, multi-dimensional characters with:

- **Basic profiles** - Names, descriptions, and core attributes
- **Personality traits** - Define how characters think and behave
- **Tags & categories** - Organize and discover characters easily
- **Knowledge & memories** - Give characters context and history

### [Setting Builder](#/docs/setting-builder)

Design immersive worlds with:

- **Location hierarchies** - Build nested areas from worlds to rooms
- **Environmental details** - Describe atmospheres and characteristics
- **World knowledge** - Establish lore and setting-specific information

### [Interactive Sessions](#/docs/sessions)

Engage with your creations through:

- **Real-time chat** - Have natural conversations with NPCs
- **Context-aware responses** - Characters remember and react to your interactions
- **Multi-agent system** - NPCs process sensory input, emotions, and reasoning

## [Getting Started](#/docs/quick-start)

1. **Run the App** - Follow the [Self-Hosting guide](#/docs/self-hosting) to start ArcAgentic locally
2. **Create a Character** - Build your first NPC in the [Character Builder](#/docs/character-builder)
3. **Design a Setting** - Craft the world your character inhabits in the [Setting Builder](#/docs/setting-builder)
4. **Start a Session** - Bring it all together and begin interacting with your characters
